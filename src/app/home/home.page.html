Our Services

Art Community

We go after supernatural creativity through art! You will learn how to co-create with God, prophecy through art, overcome fears, find inner healing, and get encouragement and accountability.

Kingdom Writers Association

You will gain a community and family of writers who love to encourage one another and provide a safe place to share your writing. Learn how God wants to use your writing gift to touch others, to prophesy, heal, and even bring you through healing.

Saturday Morning Creative Worship

Two Saturdays out of the month we gather all the creatives to worship and pray. As well as do group activations, mini teachings, and follow the Holy Spirit's lead to create, encourage one another, and have fun!

Kingdom Dance and Movement

David danced before the Lord and so shall we! Come learn more about how God redeems our bodies, break off body shame, and learn redeemed expressions. You'll learn how to use flags, dance with freedom, and how to connect to God through movement.

Workshops

God can move supernaturally through creativity. Learn how to prophecy, heal, evangelize, worship, intercede, and encounter the Lord at a weekend workshop- hosted by us or bring us to you!
